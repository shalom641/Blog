SAP (Systems, Applications, and Products in Data Processing) is a global leader in enterprise resource planning (ERP) software, enabling businesses to streamline processes and achieve digital transformation across departments.

SAP in the Digital Era

With the evolution of cloud computing and data analytics, SAP has embraced new technologies such as SAP S/4HANA to drive next-generation ERP and ensure enterprises remain competitive in the digital age.

History of SAP

Founded in 1972 by five former IBM engineers, SAP was created to enable real-time business processing and has since evolved into a leader in ERP systems globally, transforming business operations for millions of customers.
